SteamHead loves Maker Education. Making is about sharing, and sharing expertly is teaching.

We believe that the Maker movement naturally evolves towards Maker Education, and have created a space in Shenzhen, China that gives these resources to teachers. And anyone can be a teacher.

We have classes for students, study groups and free resources for teachers, along with events and professional development courses for schools.

Maker Mentors
SteamHead members who are also teachers. Their hands and hearts are tapped into Shenzhen's Maker Ed heartbeat. Full profiles on our blog!
